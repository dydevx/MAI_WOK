# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Mai Wok serves customers in Freiburg who want to browse the menu, choose dishes, send an order request through WhatsApp or email, and pick up food at the restaurant. Most users are scanning quickly on mobile before lunch, dinner, or on the way to pick up takeaway.

## Product Purpose
The website presents Mai Wok as a fresh, reliable local restaurant and makes takeaway ordering simple. Success means customers can quickly understand opening hours, view the lunch and dinner menus, add dishes to the cart, send a clear pickup request, and find the restaurant without friction.

## Brand Personality
Fresh, welcoming, refined. The interface should feel bright and appetizing, with enough warmth for a neighborhood restaurant and enough polish to feel trustworthy.

## Anti-references
Avoid dark restaurant themes, heavy black hero imagery, empty white sections, disconnected floating cards, generic beige-only palettes, and cluttered menu pages that feel like a raw PDF copied into a website.

## Design Principles
- Keep ordering first: every visual choice should make selecting dishes and sending a request easier.
- Bright appetite: use light, fresh color and real food imagery without making the site feel dark.
- Connected sections: each area should feel part of one continuous restaurant experience, not separate blocks.
- Menu clarity: names, prices, descriptions, availability, and cart state must be easy to scan.
- Trust at pickup: contact details, map, hours, and request status should feel calm and dependable.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Target WCAG AA contrast for text and controls. Keep motion subtle with reduced-motion support. Make controls usable on mobile, preserve native form behavior where helpful, and avoid relying on color alone for important states.
